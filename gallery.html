Ethiopia

From high-altitude springs to sprawling irrigation schemes, VAMA pumps power the nation’s water infrastructure.

Addis Ababa High-Rise Booster

Professional insight: Three VMS-65 stainless vertical multistage pumps coupled to 30 kW IE3 motors deliver 12 bar constant pressure to a 32-storey mixed-use tower. The system employs a PLC-controlled duty-assist-standby algorithm to optimise energy consumption and extend service life.

End-user benefit: Residents enjoy uninterrupted water at every tap—even during frequent city-wide pressure drops—while owners cut running costs by 18 % compared to the previous cast-iron units.

Awash River Solar Irrigation

Professional insight: Hybrid 8SD-60 pump with 37 kW Franklin submersible motor and MPPT inverter provides 44 l/s at 78 m head, irrigating 140 ha maize with zero diesel dependency.

Farmer impact: Crop yield +28 % after first season, water-table drawdown reduced by 12 %, ROI under 2 years.

Kenya

Reliably serving community boreholes in arid counties and pressure-boosting hotels along the coast.

Turkana Community Wells

24 off-grid 4SD-12 pumps driven by 3.7 kW brushless DC motors provide safe drinking water for 11,800 residents. Telemetry panels report daily flow and dynamic level to NGO HQ in Lodwar.

Local testimonial: “Water queues are down from four hours to twenty minutes.” — A. Ekal, village chief.

Italy

Where it all began: rigorous factory testing and pilot projects across Emilia-Romagna.

Factory Proof-Testing Facility

Every pump leaving the Cavriago plant is bench-tested against EN ISO 9906 Grade 1 tolerances. The 60-bar closed loop captures full Q-H curves, vibration spectrum and efficiency index, archived for complete traceability.

Iraq

High-efficiency multistage boosters keep desalinated water moving through kilometre-long pipelines in 45 °C heat.

Abu Dhabi District Cooling

Six VMT-32 pumps operating in parallel move 480 m³/h chilled water through a 4.2 km loop. Duplex-grade wetted parts extend life expectancy despite aggressive water chemistry.

Energy saving: VFD-driven ΔP control cuts annual consumption by 740 MWh versus fixed-speed design.

Gezira Rapid-Response Wells

Ten containerised borehole skids—each with a 6SD-45 pump, generator set and chlorination dosing—produced 11 million litres of safe water within 14 days of arrival.

NGO feedback: “Deployment was plug-and-play; field team required only a basic wrench set.” — Relief International.
