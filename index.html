About VAMA SRL

Headquartered in Reggio Emilia, Italy, VAMA SRL designs and produces submersible, vertical multistage and surface centrifugal pumps entirely from AISI stainless steel. With a flexible production line and in-house R&D, we tailor each model to regional standards and ship to municipal, industrial, agricultural and marine customers in more than 40 countries.

Know-How, Efficiency & Total Quality

From raw steel to final testing, every stage happens in Italy under one roof.

Our vertically-integrated process covers laser-cutting, deep-drawing, welding and precision balancing to guarantee corrosion-proof construction and hydraulic efficiency. VAMA’s ISO-certified labs run 100 % performance checks before a pump leaves the factory, so installers on five continents can commission systems with confidence.
