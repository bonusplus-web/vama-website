Submersible Borehole Pumps

Built on three decades of stainless-steel know-how, our submersible line delivers clean, reliable water from the chalky limestone of Dire Dawa to the basalt aquifers of Tigray.

Every impeller, diffuser and sleeve is laser-welded in Reggio Emilia, then test-run at 50 / 60 Hz so you can power the pump from diesel gensets, solar drives or the national grid without redesign. Lower energy bills, fewer breakdowns — more water, every day.

  • 4″ series — small community & domestic wells
  • 6″ series — agriculture & town water-works
  • 8″ series — high-capacity municipal supply
  • 10″ / 12″ series — large-scale irrigation & industry

Vertical Multistage Boosters

When footprint is scarce, our inline boosters tuck neatly between pipes, generating skyscraper pressure with the sipping-fuel appetite of a domestic appliance.

All wet parts are AISI 304/316; double mechanical seals keep your RO membranes pristine, while cartridge bearings allow a full seal swap in under 20 minutes — no crane required.

  • Analyze data trends for predictive maintenance

Controls & Accessories

A pump is only as smart as the panel that drives it. Our Danfoss and Siemens VFDs auto-optimise power factor, soft-start motors in <3 s and text you before a fault becomes a failure.

Need telemetry? Drop in a GSM module and view live flow, head and kWh on any phone — even over 2 G links. Downtime isn’t an option when the crop cycle can’t wait.

  • VFDs 0.75 – 90 kW with pump-optimised PID
  • Star-delta & soft-starter control panels
  • Level, pressure & flow instrumentation
  • Mechanical-seal & bearing service kits
